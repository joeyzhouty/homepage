Brief Bio:

Hi! My name is Joey Tianyi Zhou. I am a scientist，PI and group head in the Institute of High Performance Computing, at the Agency for Science, Technology, and Research (A*STAR), Singapore. I am leading the Efficient Computing Group with IHPC. Earlier, I worked as a Senior Research Engineer for Autonomous Driving Project at Sony US Research Center, San Jose, USA. I obtained my Ph.D. degree from Nanyang Technological University (NTU), SG, in 2015 Dec.
