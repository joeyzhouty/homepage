Brief Bio:

Hi! My name is Joey Tianyi Zhou (Chinese: 周天异). I am a scientist in Institute of High Performance Computing, at Agency for Science, Technology and Research (A*STAR), Singapore. Earlier, I obtained my Ph.D. degree from Nanyang Technological University (NTU), SG, in 2015 Dec, and then worked as a Senior Research Engineer at Sony US research Center, San Jose, USA.
