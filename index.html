Brief Bio:

Hi! My name is Huazhu Fu (Chinese: 付华柱 ). I am a Research Scientist in Institute for Infocomm Research, at Agency for Science, Technology and Research (A*STAR), Singapore. Earlier, I obtained my Ph.D. degree from Tianjin University, China, in 2013, and then worked as a research fellow at Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore, for two years. I am the Associate Editor of IEEE Access and BMC Medical Imaging.
